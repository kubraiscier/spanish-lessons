### Spelling

In Spanish we read what we write

Examples:

`libro`  ->  libro

`bandera` -> bandera

#### There are some rules:


Letter  | Rule          | Example
------- | ------------- | ---------
c       | c + e,i => /s/ </br> c + a,o,u => /k/ | cesar /s/ </br> cuederno  /k/    
q       | always with u (k) | quiero /kiero/      
h       | no sound          | hacienda /asienda/
j       | h                 | julio /hulio/
ch      | ç                 | churro /çurro/
ll      | y                 | llmas /yamas/
